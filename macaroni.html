Macaroni & Cheese

Ingredients

  • Kraft Macaroni box
  • Kraft Macaroni noodles
  • Kraft Macaroni powder cheese packet
  • Boiling Water

Steps

  1. Go to store
  2. Buy Kraft Mac & Cheese
  3. Drive home *or method of transportation*
  4. Boil water in pot
  5. Add Kraft Macaroni
  6. Wait 7 - 8 minutes
  7. Drain Macaroni and place back in pot
  8. Add Kraft cheese packet
  9. Stir cheese with macaroni
  10. Enjoy tasty Macaroni & Cheese
